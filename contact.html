Prospective students and postdocs

I am happy to hear from prospective MSc and PhD students whose research interests align with our work. Please read the About page and look at our papers before reaching out. Keep in mind that I am primarily looking for students interested in bacterial genomics and evolution.

Please note that international MSc students receive substantially less funding support than domestic students at UNB, which limits my ability to take them on. International PhD students at UNB are not subject to the same constraints.

I am unlikely to have (unadvertised) funding to fully support postdoctoral researchers, but I would be happy to collaborate on Postdoctoral Fellowship applications, and to discuss possibilities!
